The brightest light casts the darkest shadows. Technological innovations since the industrial revolution have been one of the greatest lights in our history, casting the murkiest shadow of climate change to envelope over the planet of Earth. Daniel Yergin beautifully unfolds the grand history of climate change dating back from Tyndalls masterpiece of icy glaciers to the global controversies surrounding hot political disputes. In response to the climate change, the idea of emission trade was proposed
