This book vividly conveys how the content revolution is taking place and what changes it will bring to the front line of the revolution.In the era of the content revolution, content is constantly moving, wriggling, and recreating through consumer interaction. What the author is paying attention to is the consumer-centered paradigm at the base of all these revolutionary changes. The core of data-driven content services is also respect for consumers' needs and choices.The value and competitiveness of content depending on how well consumers' propensity and needs are understood and reflected. In the era of the content revolution, value is created from the experience of consuming content rather than the content itself. The reason why virtual reality VR and augmented reality AR are emerging is because they provide new user experiences that have never been before by dramatically improving the consumer experience.
